Monochromatic Matchings in Ordered Complete Graphs

Consider an ordered, 2-colored complete graph. Edges are either "nested", "separated", or "crossing". We study extremal questions in this domain. The figure demonstrates that \(R_{\text{nest}}(M_2,M_3) > 5\). In this project, we strengthen a bound on \(R_{\text{nest}}(M_k,M_k)\) and look at the \(t\)-coloring variant of the problem.

Finalized Poster and Manuscript expected: 5/2025
Enumerating Maximal Independent Sets in Grid-like Graphs

Introduced a simple framework of states to count maximal independent sets on a class of grid-like graphs with the number of walks on a state digraph, studying asymptotics. We strengthen bounds on the Maximal Hard Square Entropy Constant - the exponential rate of growth of the spectral radii of state digraphs of a subclass of our graphs. We also explore non-isomorphic enumeration.

Finalized Manuscript expected: 12/2024 View our poster. Come see us at 2025 JMM!
Tree Augmentation Implementation

Given a tree \(T=(V,E)\) and a set of candidate links \(L\), find a minimum set \(L' \subseteq L\) such that \((V,E\cup L')\) is 2-edge connected. When \(L \cup E\) is not complete, the problem is \(\textbf{NP-complete}\). We contrast the performance of four published approximation algorithms against our naive randomized algorithm.

Finalized Manuscript expected: 12/2024
